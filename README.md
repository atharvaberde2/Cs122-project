How to Run:

Run the main method in app.py, and it will tell you on which socket address it is running on. Click the link, and it will take you to the home page on your browser. 

If you want to experiment creating a new
budget, play with the different fields, preferably your information, and you will get an accurate prediction of what your monthly spending budget should look like. 
Using scikit-learn and random forest 
regression model, we can train 80% of exisiting data from customer_data.csv to teach the model and 20% of unseen data to firmly predict the model against actual values and a monthly budget for new users. 

In the next section, multiple users can record new expenses and income transactions to track their spending and earning habits over time. 
Each user's transactions will be updated to their total worth, and they
will be able to see their cumulative expense line chart by differing personal budget categories, including food, transportation, healthcare, entertainment, and utilities.
